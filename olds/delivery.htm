Delivery Information

Standard Delivery

Orders for $100 or more are delivered FREE of charge. Orders under $100 will have a $10.99 delivery charge.

Delivery Lead Time

Orders are usually dispatched within 24-48 hours and should arrive within 3-4 working days.

Tracking Your Order

A tracking number is provided the same afternoon your order is dispatched. Your order can be tracked online after you receive your tracking number.
